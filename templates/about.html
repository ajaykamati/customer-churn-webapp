About This Project

Customer Churn Prediction System

This application is designed to predict whether a customer will stop doing business with a company (churn). Customer churn is a critical metric for businesses, as acquiring new customers is often much more expensive than retaining existing ones.

Machine Learning Model

We use an advanced XGBoost Classifier trained on historical customer data. The model analyzes 9 key customer features including Age, Relationships, Credit Limit, and Transaction activity to calculate the probability of a customer churning.

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Python, Flask, Pandas, Scikit-learn, XGBoost
  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 5
